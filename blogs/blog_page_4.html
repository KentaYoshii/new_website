My Personal Blog

In Japan, the option of studying abroad after high school is not something that everyone considers. I was like that too until I was introduced to it from someone who I met outside of my school. Through writing blogs and sharing my student experience at Brown University, I want the readers to get the sense of what it is like to study in a foreign country and start to consider that option for themselves. Since 2017, I have been working as a blog writer with Sakae Institute of Study Abroad. Here are the list of blog entries I created. They are all in Japanese.
